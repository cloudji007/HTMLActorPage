Balaji

There are two people named Balaji that are commonly known. Balaji Vishwanath was the 6th Peshwa of the Maratha Empire and is called the ‘Second founder of the Maratha State’. Lord Balaji, also known as Venkateswara, Tirupati Thimmappa, and Govinda, is an avatar of Lord Vishnu and is revered by Hindus. He is worshipped in the form of a granite stone idol and is located in the holy Seven Hills.
